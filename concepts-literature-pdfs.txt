What is Music Interaction?
The term Music Interaction specifically refers to “Music and Human-Computer Interaction,” which, according to leaders in the field, “encompasses the design, refinement, evaluation, analysis and use of interactive systems that involve computer technology for any kind of musical activity” (Holland et al., 2013)
Holland, S., Wilkie, K., Mulholland, P., & Seago, A. (2013). Music interaction: Understanding music and human-computer interaction. In Music and Human-Computer Interaction (pp. 1-28). Springer London.
